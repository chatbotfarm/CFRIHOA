Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-06

CFRIHOA (“CFRIHOA,” “we,” “us,” “our”) is a local residential rental provider. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1) Information We Collect

  • Contact information (name, phone number, email address, mailing address).
  • Rental inquiry and application info you provide (messages, appointment requests, documents you submit).
  • Resident communications (maintenance requests, service updates, scheduling details).
  • Website and device data (IP address, browser type, pages viewed, approximate location derived from IP).

2) How We Use Information

  • To respond to inquiries, schedule viewings, and provide rental information.
  • To operate resident services (maintenance coordination, notifications, reminders, service updates).
  • To communicate by phone, email, and SMS/text message when you request or consent to those communications.
  • To improve our website, services, and resident experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce policies.

3) SMS/Text Messaging (A2P/10DLC Notice)

If you provide your phone number and opt in (or request service updates by text), you agree to receive SMS/text messages from CFRIHOA. Messages may include rental inquiries, viewing coordination, application links, resident service communications, maintenance updates, and account-related notices.

  • Message frequency varies based on your interaction and service needs.
  • Message & data rates may apply depending on your carrier and plan.
  • Help: Reply HELP for assistance.
  • Opt-out: Reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe from SMS messages.
  • After you reply STOP, you may receive a final confirmation message and then no further SMS will be sent unless you opt in again.
  • Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

4) Your Choices and Unsubscribe Options

  • SMS: Reply STOP to unsubscribe; reply START to re-subscribe (where supported).
  • Email: Use the unsubscribe link in marketing emails (if present) or request removal by contacting us.
  • Account/Service communications: Some communications are necessary for service delivery (e.g., maintenance scheduling). You can request alternate contact methods.

5) How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers who help us operate (e.g., hosting, analytics, communications/SMS platforms, CRM tools, and maintenance coordination), but only as needed to provide our services and subject to confidentiality obligations.

We may also disclose information if required by law, to protect rights and safety, or in connection with a business transaction (e.g., reorganization), consistent with applicable law.

6) Data Retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to provide services, meet legal requirements, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Retention periods vary depending on the type of data and purpose.

7) Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8) Cookies and Analytics

We may use cookies or similar technologies to operate our site and understand usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Some site features may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

9) Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
